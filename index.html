Taipei, Taiwan — December 2025

Ichiran's solo-booth tonkotsu ramen

Ichiran Ramen (一蘭拉麵)

Rich, creamy tonkotsu broth with a velvety mouthfeel. The thin noodles stayed firm through the whole bowl, and the chashu practically melted. What stood out most was the fresh scallion — its sharp, clean aroma cut through the richness with every bite, balanced against the secret red chili sauce.
